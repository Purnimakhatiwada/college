NIHAREEKA COLLEGE OF IT AND MANAGEMENT

College of IT and Management is a growing and trusted educational institution located in Biratnagar-19, Bargachhi. The college offers strong academic programs in Information Technology and Management, supported by experienced faculty and a student-friendly environment. With a focus on practical learning, modern computer labs, and interactive teaching methods, Nihareeka helps students build real-world skills and confidence. The college is known for its discipline, supportive guidance, and commitment to preparing students for professional careers in the fields of IT and management.

  • Bachelor of Computer Aplication(BCA)
  • Bsc csit
  • Bachelor in Business Studies(BBS)
